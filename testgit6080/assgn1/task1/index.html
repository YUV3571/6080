The AI Bubble May Be Deflating

Tech leaders are lining up behind the next big trend.

COMP6080 8 min read · Jan, 2026

Speculation now dominates the global economy. It wasn't always this way. From the 1980s through to 2008, a shift occurred: investors discovered that hype could generate far greater returns than building sustainable, productive businesses. In the information age, where narratives are easily shaped, manipulated, and sold, this was almost inevitable. The result has been a succession of speculative booms—the dot-com bubble, the 2008 financial crisis, the 2016–2017 cryptocurrency surge.
